Ling Wang | 汪羚


I'm currently a PhD student (2022-) at Department of Computer Science, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof.Fuchun Sun. I obtained my B.S. and M.S at Xi'an Research Institute of Hi-Tech.

My research interest lies in the 3D Reconstruction, 3D Generation, and Robotics. I am particularly interested in combining Vision Language Action models with 3D generation methods.
